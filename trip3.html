Mahabaleshwar

2-day trip

Package : 20,000 Rs

Mahabaleshwar, located in the Western Ghats of Maharashtra, is a popular hill station known for its scenic beauty and pleasant weather. Here's a concise tourist guide:

  1. Natural Attractions: Explore the breathtaking views at Wilson Point, the highest peak, and Elephant's Head Point, which resembles an elephant's trunk. Visit Arthur's Seat for mesmerizing valley views.

  2. Waterfalls : Don't miss Lingmala Waterfall, Dhobi Waterfall, and Chinaman's Waterfall, especially during the monsoon.

  3. Temples :Visit the ancient Mahabaleshwar Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, and the Panchganga Temple, where five rivers converge.

  4. Venna Lake: Enjoy boating, horse riding, and delicious street food around this serene lake.

  5. Strawberry Farms: Mahabaleshwar is famous for its strawberries. Visit farms for fresh strawberries and try strawberry-based treats.

  6. Mapro Garden: A must-visit spot for strawberry products, pizzas, and desserts, perfect for food lovers.

  7. Adventure: Try trekking and nature walks in the lush greenery or take a scenic drive through Pratapgad Fort, a historic site nearby.

  8. Shopping: Explore the local markets for leather goods, handicrafts, and fresh produce like strawberries, mulberries, and jams.

  9. Best Time to Visit: Ideal during winter (November to February) or early summer (March to May) for pleasant weather.

  10. Local Delicacies: Don't miss the famous chikki (a traditional sweet) and authentic Maharashtrian cuisine.

  11. Recent Updates: Lonavala continues to be a popular weekend getaway, and the local authorities maintain tourist hotspots like Bushi Dam and Karla Caves. Crowds are common during weekends and holidays, so plan accordingly to avoid peak hours.
Enjoy your trip to Mahabaleshwar and make the most of its natural charm and cultural heritage!
