Here's some useful tourist information about Lonavala to enhance your trip:
Location & Accessibility: Lonavala is a hill station in Maharashtra, located about 65 km from Pune and 90 km from Mumbai. It is accessible via the Mumbai-Pune Expressway, offering scenic views of the Western Ghats.
Best Time to Visit: While Lonavala is beautiful year-round, the post-monsoon and winter seasons (October to March) are ideal due to pleasant weather and lush greenery.
Key Attractions :

  1. Bhushi Dam: A must-visit spot, especially during monsoons, with its overflowing water and scenic surroundings.

  2. Karla and Bhaja Caves: Ancient Buddhist rock-cut caves with intricate carvings and historical significance.

  3. Tiger's Leap & Lion's Point: Spectacular viewpoints offering panoramic views of valleys and sunsets.

  4. Della Adventure Park: Perfect for adrenaline seekers, offering activities like zip-lining, bungee jumping, and ATV rides.

  5. Pawna Lake: A serene spot for camping, picnicking, and stargazing.

  6. Rajmachi Fort: Popular for trekking and its historical charm.

Activities: Enjoy nature walks, trekking to forts, camping by Pawna Lake, or indulging in thrilling adventures at Della Adventure Park.

Local Delicacies: Don't miss the famous chikki (a traditional sweet) and authentic Maharashtrian cuisine.

Lonavala continues to be a popular weekend getaway, and the local authorities maintain tourist hotspots like Bushi Dam and Karla Caves. Crowds are common during weekends and holidays, so plan accordingly to avoid peak hours.
