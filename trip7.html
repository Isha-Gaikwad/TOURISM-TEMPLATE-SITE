Nagpur

2-day trip

Package : 20,000 Rs

Here's a concise tourist guide to Nagpur:

  1. Deekshabhoomi:Deekshabhoomi, Asia's largest stupa and a site of historic mass Buddhist conversions, draws global devotees, with nearby Ram Dham enhancing its appeal.

  2. Ambazari lake: flanked by mango trees, offers boating, games, a 25-acre garden, and diverse flora and fauna, making it a must-visit in Nagpur.

  3. Japenese Rose Garden:It is a man-made garden with an exquisite layout and arrangement of flowers and plants. The flowers and trails have been meticulously organised to provide the park's visitors with a beautiful experience.

  4. Raman Science Centre:It is an interactive science centre that hosts science exhibitions and fosters scientific attitudes and the expansion of science and technology in many industries and people's livelihoods.

  5. Futala Lake:Lake features stunning fountains, an immersion ghat, local food vendors, and lakeside restaurants, making it a vibrant spot for family outings and romantic evenings.

  6. Sitabuldi fort:This fort is one of Nagpur's most popular attractions and an excellent place to visit.

  7. Markanda:The city of Markanda should be on your bucket list, especially if you enjoy mythology, traditions, art museums, and storytelling.

  8. Sakkardara Lake: Lake Garden is noted for its sparkling beauty and lovely gardens, making it the ideal place to escape the city's clamour and commotion to relax and unwind.

  9. Balaji Temple:a notable spiritual tourist destination in Nagpur, is a pilgrimage site dedicated to Lord Balaji and is nestled atop the magnificent Seminary Hills.

  10. Maharaj Bagh and Zoo: a one-day picnic here is a great way to enjoy the beauty of this garden and a respite for all nature lovers.

Explore these places to experience Nagpur's vibrant culture, history, and food!
