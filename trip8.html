Chandrapur

2-day trip

Package : 20,000 Rs

Here's a concise tourist guide to Chandrapur:

  1. Mahakali Temple:The inhabitants of Chandrapur consider it to be among the city's most important landmarks.

  2. Bhadravati Jain Temple: The Central Railway will take you 32 kilometres to the settlement of Bhadravati, where you'll find the temple.

  3. Anchaleshwar Mahadev Temple:This is a limestone monument that showcases the mastery of ancient decorators via its ornate carvings and embellishments.

  4. Tadoba National Park:the oldest and largest national park in Maharashtra, is one of India's 47 tiger reserves.

  5. Ballarpur Fort:Ballarpur Fort can be seen near Ballarpur town, which is around 16 kilometres from Chandrapur.

  6. Manikgad Fort:Manigarh, a hill fort constructed by Naga monarchs in the 9th century, is located 507 metres above sea level.

  7. Chandrapur Fort:This fort was constructed primarily as a defence mechanism to shield the monarch and his soldiers from hostile assaults.

Explore these places to experience Chandrapur's vibrant culture, history, and wildlife!
