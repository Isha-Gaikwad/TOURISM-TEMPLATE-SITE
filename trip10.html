Amravati

2-day trip

Package : 20,000 Rs

Amravati, located in Maharashtra, is known for its rich cultural heritage and historical significance. The city is home to ancient temples like the Ambadevi Temple and beautiful parks, making it a blend of spirituality and nature. It also serves as a key educational and administrative hub in the Vidarbha region.

  1. Shri Ambadevi Temple:Shree Ambadevi Temple in Amravati is an ancient and revered shrine dedicated to Goddess Ambadevi, attracting devotees for its spiritual significance and historic charm.

  2. Bamboo Garden: The Bamboo Garden in Amravati is a unique ecological park showcasing diverse bamboo species, offering visitors a serene and educational experience amidst nature.

  3. Melghat Tiger Reserve:Melghat Tiger Reserve, located in Amravati, is a renowned wildlife sanctuary known for its rich biodiversity and as a vital habitat for tigers and other endangered species.

  4. Woods water park Resort:Woods Water Park Resort in Amravati is a popular getaway offering water slides, pools, and recreational activities, perfect for family outings and weekend retreats.

  5. Devi Point:Devi Point in Amravati is a serene viewpoint offering panoramic views of the surrounding hills and valleys, making it a favorite spot for nature lovers and tourists.

  6. Wadali talao:Wadali Talao in Amravati is a tranquil lake surrounded by lush greenery, offering boating and a peaceful retreat for nature enthusiasts and visitors.

  7. Malkhed dam:Malkhed Dam, located near Amravati, is a scenic reservoir that provides irrigation and serves as a popular spot for nature lovers and picnickers.

  8. Chilkahrada Wild Life Sanctuary:Chilkahrada Wildlife Sanctuary near Amravati is a scenic reserve, rich in biodiversity, providing a peaceful environment for wildlife lovers and nature enthusiasts.
